KAY MRP II - An integrated manufacturing Solution for Pharma Industries

Kay MRP is a comprehensive software solution exclusively developed by Kay to address the needs of pharmaceutical companies. It has been conceived and designed keeping the unique requirements of pharmaceutical industry thereby enabling easy customization and quick implementation.

Kay MRP completely automizes and covers all the department of a Pharmaceutical company. It creates an integration between the dept. decreasing the work load and enabling fast access of data in real time environment. The system starts from Sales forecast , Purchases , Material receiving , Production control that includes both manufacturing and packaging , quality control till the sales of the product. It deals with toll manufacturing and all the features that might be possible in Pharma industry . The system promises for any information that could be require to have quick and right decisions for running an industry with maximum ouput .

The system work on network provided with different level of user & password facility and is very user friendly.

Kay MRP has a modular architecture and comes with a choice of modules built around the core suite of manufacturing and business related modules. Hence if a company want to computerize a single sector like Purchase , Warehouse or Production we can offer separate software modules for them that could be integrated with our other software modules when desire.

Kay MRP is running successfully in Eli-Lilly since 1999, implemented in Getz Pharma in year 2001 , in Hilton Pharma year 2003 and in Opal Laboratories since 2004.
