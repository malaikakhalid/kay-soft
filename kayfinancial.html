KAY FINANCIAL SYSTEM

Kay Financial System fully covers all the aspect of company accounts. It starts from Purchases and ends on Sales. With reference to Purchase module system maintain Supplier information with multiple tax structure and chart of accounts link, generate Purchase order , perform automatic tax calculation , receipt of purchases that update stock & manage asset , supplier payable, supplier billing , Payments , Cost accounting and Expense management . On the other hand system maintain Sales order management that includes Order management , D/C & Invoices , Tax management , Product inventory , Sales return , Debit & Credit Notes . System offers Asset management and auto depreciation calculation .Payroll cover complete HR module. System provides unlimited level for chart of accounts.

The system is secured with password facility and cover all the Tax accounting and Financial reports as Trial balance , Balance Sheet , Profit & Loss Statement , Cash Book , Bank Book , all types of sales report , all types of ledger either Accounts ledger , Customer ledger or Product/item ledger . System offers voucher printing and cheque printing . The reports generated by the system can be sent by email or converted in Excel format, similarly to avoid data entry bulk of data can be uploaded in the system with single click. System is designed in such a way to input minimum data entry and output maximum reports. The system is provided with Multi currency and Post dated cheque facility. The system can be customize according to user requirement and promises for all reports that may require by the user.

When integrated with Kay MRP-II it controls Material stock both quantity wise and valuation wise start from the purchase till the complete utilization & offers you reports as aging, purchase variance , valuation wise bin card and many more . It controls Product stock start from production order till batch costing and product sales .
